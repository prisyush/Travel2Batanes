Payuhan Festival

The Payuhan Festival is a lively celebration in Itbayat, Batanes, honoring the town’s bountiful harvest and the resilient spirit of the Ivatan people. It reflects gratitude for nature’s blessings and community cooperation.

Meaning of "Payuhan"

“Payuhan” comes from the Ivatan word for abundance or plentiful harvest, emphasizing thankfulness and sharing.

What to Expect

• Traditional Ivatan dances, songs, and performances
• Display of local crafts and culinary specialties
• Community feast featuring seasonal crops and seafood
• Rituals and prayers for prosperity
• Fun activities and games for locals and visitors

Why Visit During Payuhan Festival?

• Witness the Ivatan way of celebrating harvest and life
• Enjoy local delicacies and cultural performances
• Experience the warm and welcoming Ivatan community
• Discover unique traditions of Itbayat and its people
The Payuhan Festival is a vibrant celebration of Ivatan culture, nature’s abundance, and community spirit.
