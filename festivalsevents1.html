Vunung Festival

The Vunung Festival is a traditional Ivatan celebration held in Sabtang, Batanes. It is a thanksgiving festival that shows the strong faith, unity, and rich culture of the Ivatan people.

Meaning of "Vunung"

“Vunung” means togetherness or unity. The festival highlights cooperation among families and the community.

What to Expect

• Traditional Ivatan dances and music
• Cultural performances and rituals
• Community feast with local dishes
• Showcasing of Ivatan traditions and heritage
• Religious thanksgiving activities

Why Visit During Vunung Festival?

• Experience authentic Ivatan culture
• Witness preserved traditions passed through generations
• Enjoy warm hospitality of the locals
• Take part in meaningful community celebrations
The Vunung Festival is a beautiful reminder of how the Ivatan people value faith, culture, and unity in their daily lives.
