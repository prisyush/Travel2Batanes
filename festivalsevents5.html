Palu-Palo Festival

The Palu-Palo Festival is a vibrant celebration in Sabtang, Batanes, honoring the harvest season and the hardworking spirit of the Ivatan people. It highlights the town’s agricultural abundance and the importance of community cooperation.

Meaning of "Palu-Palo"

“Palu-Palo” refers to the traditional method of pounding harvested crops, symbolizing hard work, unity, and gratitude for nature’s blessings.

What to Expect

• Traditional dances and music performances
• Demonstrations of Ivatan farming practices
• Display of local produce and agricultural products
• Community feast featuring seasonal harvests
• Fun games, parades, and cultural activities

Why Visit During Palu-Palo Festival?

• Witness the Ivatan way of celebrating harvest and community life
• Experience local customs and agricultural traditions
• Enjoy fresh local produce and dishes
• Take part in lively cultural activities and parades
The Palu-Palo Festival is a joyful celebration of Ivatan heritage, agriculture, and the strong bond of the community.
