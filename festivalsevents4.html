Vakul-Kanayi Festival

The Vakul-Kanayi Festival is a unique celebration in Basco, Batanes, honoring the traditional Ivatan clothing and craftsmanship. It showcases the iconic vakul (headgear) and kanayi (outerwear), symbols of Ivatan resilience against the region’s harsh weather.

Meaning of "Vakul-Kanayi"

The festival is named after the traditional garments vakul and kanayi, reflecting Ivatan ingenuity, culture, and daily life.

What to Expect

• Fashion shows featuring traditional Ivatan attire
• Demonstrations of weaving and garment-making
• Cultural performances and parades
• Exhibits on Ivatan heritage and craftsmanship
• Activities highlighting Ivatan traditions and lifestyle

Why Visit During Vakul-Kanayi Festival?

• Learn about the Ivatan people’s traditional clothing and culture
• Witness live demonstrations of local craftsmanship
• Enjoy colorful parades and cultural performances
• Appreciate the rich heritage and resilience of Ivatan community
The Vakul-Kanayi Festival is a celebration of Ivatan tradition, creativity, and the enduring spirit of the people of Batanes.
