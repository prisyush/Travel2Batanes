Vunes

Vunes is a traditional Ivatan dish made from dried gabi (taro) leaves. The leaves are carefully dried under the sun and later cooked with garlic, onions, and sometimes minced pork or fish. Coconut milk may also be added to enhance its flavor.

Taste & Texture

• Creamy and slightly earthy
• Savory with a mild natural bitterness
• Soft and smooth when cooked

Why It's Special

• Uses locally grown taro leaves
• A practical way of preserving vegetables in Batanes
• Healthy and rich in nutrients
• Reflects the simple and sustainable Ivatan lifestyle

Where to Try in Batanes

• Local Ivatan restaurants
• Small eateries in Basco
• Traditional family-run carinderias

Travel Tip

Vunes is best enjoyed warm with steamed rice and grilled fish. For a full Ivatan meal experience, pair it with other local favorites like Uved or Luñis!
