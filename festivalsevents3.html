Kulay Festival

The Kulay Festival is a colorful celebration in Basco, Batanes, that highlights Ivatan art, creativity, and cultural heritage. It showcases the talent and vibrant traditions of the local community through art, colors, and performances.

Meaning of "Kulay"

“Kulay” means color in Filipino, symbolizing the vibrancy of Ivatan culture and the lively spirit of the community.

What to Expect

• Art exhibits and cultural performances
• Street parades filled with colorful costumes and decorations
• Workshops on Ivatan crafts and traditional arts
• Music, dance, and cultural presentations
• Activities for visitors to engage with local culture

Why Visit During Kulay Festival?

• Experience the artistic and cultural richness of Batanes
• Witness vibrant parades and performances
• Participate in interactive art and craft activities
• Enjoy the lively, festive atmosphere of Basco
The Kulay Festival is a celebration of creativity, tradition, and the joyful spirit of the Ivatan people.
